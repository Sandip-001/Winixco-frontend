Privacy Policy

Scope. This Privacy Policy explains how Winixco collects, uses, discloses, and protects personal data of users of our AI-powered ATS and SaaS products worldwide.

1. Data We Collect

  • Account & Contact Data: name, company, business email, phone number, billing address, job title.
  • Credentials: username, password (stored securely — hashed).
  • Payment & Billing Data: payment instrument identifiers (only as needed for billing) — note: we use PCI-compliant third-party payment processors and do not store full card numbers on our servers unless required by a certified provider.
  • Usage & Analytics: platform usage logs, feature usage, API keys usage metadata, session times, IP address, device and browser metadata, and audit logs (for admin/customer security and compliance).
  • Recruitment Data (if uploaded): CVs/resumes, candidate contact details, interview notes, assessment results and other recruitment-related data that customers upload to the platform. Customers remain the data controllers for applicant data; Winixco acts as a data processor under applicable laws.
  • Support & Communications: support emails, chat transcripts, feedback, and survey responses.

2. How We Use Your Data

We process personal data to:

  • Provide, operate and maintain the Service;
  • Process subscriptions, invoices, refunds, and payments;
  • Allow customers to onboard, administer accounts, and manage hiring workflows;
  • Improve, personalize and develop features (including AI models and candidate-matching algorithms) using aggregated/anonymized data where possible;
  • Detect and prevent fraud, abuse or security incidents;
  • Comply with legal obligations and respond to lawful requests.

3. Legal Bases for Processing (where applicable)

For users in jurisdictions that require a legal basis (e.g., EU):

  • Contractual necessity: processing required to deliver the Service and fulfil contracts.
  • Legitimate interests: platform security, fraud prevention, and improving services (balanced against individual rights).
  • Consent: where processing (e.g., marketing emails) relies on explicit consent.

4. Sharing & Disclosures

  • We share data with service providers (payment processors, cloud hosting, analytics, email delivery, identity providers) under written contracts requiring confidentiality and limiting use to service delivery.
  • Customer-uploaded applicant data: Winixco processes this data at the direction of our customers; we will not access or use it except to provide the Service, to comply with law, or to protect the rights and safety of users.
  • Legal obligations: we may disclose data to comply with laws, lawsuits, government requests, or to protect rights, safety, or property.

5. Data Retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary to provide the Service or to comply with legal obligations. Customers may export or request deletion of their uploaded recruitment data in accordance with their account and service agreement.

6. Security

We implement administrative, technical and physical safeguards (encryption in transit and at rest, access controls, monitoring) to protect data. However, no system is 100% secure — if a security incident affecting personal data occurs, we will notify affected parties and regulators as required by law.

7. International Transfers

Winixco operates globally. Personal data may be transferred to countries with different data protection laws. We protect transfers with appropriate safeguards (standard contractual clauses, intra-group agreements, or other lawful mechanisms).

8. Your Rights & Controls

  • Access, rectify, erase, restrict or object to processing, and data portability — where applicable under local law.
  • Withdraw consent to marketing communications at any time.
  • To exercise rights, contact: support@winixco.com.

9. Children

Our Service is not directed to children under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children; please contact us to request removal if you believe a child has provided information.
